Sheikh Abdullah Nasir

Senior Quran & Hadith Instructor

Biography

Sheikh Abdullah Nasir is a renowned Islamic scholar with over 15 years of teaching experience. He graduated from the prestigious Al-Azhar University with specialization in Quranic Sciences and Hadith. The Sheikh holds Ijazah in Hafs 'an Asim and Warsh 'an Nafi recitation styles, and is authorized to teach Sahih Bukhari and Muslim.

Education & Certifications

  • PhD in Hadith Sciences - Al-Azhar University, Cairo (2015)
  • Masters in Quranic Exegesis - Islamic University of Medina (2010)
  • Ijazah in Hafs and Warsh recitation - Certified by Sheikh Mahmoud Khalil Al-Hussary

Areas of Expertise
